Water

Description

Water is the only true beverage. This guide will show you step by step how to make it.

Ingredients

  • Ice cubes
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Add the ice cubes to an appropriately sized pot on a stovetop set to Medium heat.
  2. As the water boils, capture the steam into a distillery set
  3. Wait for the water to condense and drop down as liquid water
  4. Enjoy while warm. To prevent spoilage, pour the water into ice cube tray to freeze
